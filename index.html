📖 HOW TO PLAY
🎲 THE CORE LOOP
A run is 10 levels. Each level has 3 rounds. After every level you visit the Underground Shop. Beat the Big Bookie at level 10 to win.
EACH ROUND
#STEPWHAT HAPPENS
1Pick a betChoose from 6 randomly generated events. Each has odds, a win %, and a risk tier.
2Set wagerHow much of your bankroll to risk. Win % × odds = expected value.
3Stage a cardOptional. Tap a card from your hand. It fires when you confirm the bet — not before.
4Place betCard fires, wager deducted, simulation runs.
5PayoutWIN: wager × odds returned. LOSS: wager gone. PUSH: wager returned, no profit.

🎰 THE DEGENERATE
Start: $75 · Luck: 1/10 · Perk: Free Parlay each level
Hardest early game. $75 vs a $100 ante with Luck 1 is brutal. You rely on the Free Parlay to punch above your weight.
Strategy: Play LOW/MED rounds 1–2 to survive. Round 3: use the Free Parlay on a HIGH or EXTREME bet. A 5× bet + 1.5× Parlay = 7.5× effective payout. High variance, high ceiling.
🧮 THE MATH WHIZ
Start: $300 · Luck: 4/10 · Perk: Odds Calculator (+5% on LOW/MED bets)
Best for learning. Starts with Tipster joker (+5% all bets) and Inside Info card. Odds Calculator reveals true win % permanently on LOW/MED bets.
Strategy: Never bet more than 25% of bankroll. Stack LOW/MED bets with Tipster. Use Inside Info before committing to anything MED or higher.
🤞 THE LUCKY IDIOT
Start: $100 · Luck: 10/10 · Perk: Beginner's Luck (wins pay ×1.5–×3.0 randomly)
Strategy: Go for HIGH and EXTREME bets. Luck 10 = +7.5% win chance over baseline. The random payout multiplier means a 5× bet that wins could effectively pay 15×. Lucky Socks joker adds +2 more Luck.
The Lucky Idiot has the highest single-run ceiling but the widest variance. Feast or famine.

♾️ JOKERS — PASSIVE, MAX 3 EQUIPPED

🔗 STRONG SYNERGIES
The Grinder: Tipster + Hot Streak + Lucky Socks — play LOW bets to build a win streak, then cash in the streak bonus on a HIGH bet round 3.
The Longshot King: Bookie's Grudge + Hot Streak + Parlay Slips in hand — a 6× bet becomes effectively 18× with grudge doubling, streak bonus, and parlay. One hit clears most antes.
The Safe House: Loan Shark + Inside Man + any win% booster — keep bankroll above $200, collect double interest, reveal odds for free each level, bet conservatively.
Danger Build: Brass Knuckles + Deal Shades = net +10% win, +10 Heat per loss instead of +20. The win boost is significant but heat will eventually catch up. Don't skip wins.
💡 GENERAL PRINCIPLES
Bankroll management matters more than win rate. Losing 50% of your bankroll on one bet is catastrophic. Losing 10% three times in a row is survivable.
The shop reroll cost scales up. Don't reroll more than twice per shop unless you desperately need a specific joker. The Accountant goon makes this even worse.
Cards are not disposable. Playing a card removes it from your hand. Hold Pocket Sand and The Hedge for the rounds you actually need them.
High Heat is a death spiral. Goons strip your tools. Stripped tools lower your win %. Lower win rate means more losses. More losses = more Heat. Keep Heat under 60.

🔁 LEVEL 1 SURVIVAL
The hardest ante to hit is level 1 ($100). Start bankrolls are $75–$300 depending on archetype. Math Whiz is trivially safe. Degenerate is tight.
As Degenerate with $75: bet $20 on LOW round 1 (60%+ win), $20 on LOW round 2, then dump remaining + Free Parlay on a MED+ bet round 3. If both LOW bets win you'll have ~$110–$120 heading into round 3.
👺 BOSS FIGHT — LEVELS 5 & 10
Boss fights replace the normal betting round. Instead of picking a sporting event, the boss asks you absurd yes/no questions — you bet on the answer. 4–5 rounds, HP bars for both sides.
HOW IT WORKS
PLAYER WINS ROUNDBOSS WINS ROUND
HP effectBoss takes damagePlayer takes damage
Cash effectWin based on chosen oddsLose 50% of boss wager
Wager sizeAuto-set to 10% of bankroll
Your base win chance per boss round is: 40% + (Luck × 3%) + card bonuses. At Luck 10 that's 70% base — the Lucky Idiot is actually the best boss fighter.
BOSS REWARDS
BOSSREWARD ON WINPENALTY ON LOSE
👺 Bookie Junior (Lvl 5)+$200 · Bluff card−30% bankroll
👹 The Big Bookie (Lvl 10)+$600 · Loaded Dice joker · YOU WIN−30% bankroll
Losing a boss doesn't end the run. You take a 30% penalty and the run continues. You can lose the level 5 boss and still win the game.
Cards carry into boss fights. Stage Bluff or Sure Thing before answering a boss question — it applies exactly like a normal round. The Fixer joker also works here.
